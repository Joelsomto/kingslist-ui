## 📊 Understanding Delivery Status

```
✓ DELIVERED     = Message successfully received by person
✗ FAILED        = Delivery attempt failed
⏳ PENDING       = Waiting to be sent
❓ PARTIAL      = Some people in campaign received it
✅ COMPLETED    = All people in campaign received it (100%)
```
